Submissions

Please follow the guidelines for submissions pertaining to your submission type listed below. Each submission should be sent individually. All submissions will recieve a confirmation of recieval within two days.

Manuscripts Photos

Authors should prepare manuscripts according to guidelines established in the Publication Manual of the American Psychology Association (5th ed.). The Whitman Journal of Psychology reserves the right to modify APA style. Manuscripts should be no longer than 15 pages and should include an abstract.

Additionally, all manuscripts must include a list of references as well as parenthetical documentation in accordance with APA style. It is suggested that manuscripts include the following sections: introduction, methods, results and discussion. Manuscripts are not limited to these sections. Please be sure to use academic and professional sources while conducting your research. Many university, public and your school libraries offer both printed and online sources from professional journals.

All manuscripts submitted are subject to editing on the basis of style as well as content. It is the author’s responsibility to ensure clarity of expression.

Submissions should include a cover letter in which the author’s name, school affiliation, advisor’s name, address, phone number and e-mail address are given.

Photos must be submitted in high quality and in portrait orientation.
